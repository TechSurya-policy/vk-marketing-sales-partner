Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 23, 2026

Welcome to VK Marketing (Sales Partner). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal, device, and spatial data collected through our mobile application.

VK Marketing is an operational enterprise tool for field sales partners to manage daily shop visits, order placement, route tracking, payment logs, and sales ledger statements.

Prominent Disclosure: Location & Camera Data Usage

To enable core field sales and logistics operations, VK Marketing collects and processes high-accuracy GPS location data and camera image captures even when the app is running in the background or foreground.

  • GPS Location: Used continuously to plot optimized sales routes, track sales partner locations during working hours, and verify shop visits.
  • Camera & Media: Used solely to allow sales representatives to upload custom profile pictures, take photos of stamped bills, and capture physical receipt documents.

1. Information We Collect & Permission Usages

A. Precise GPS Location Data

We request access to ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION and ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION permissions on your mobile device. This data is used to:

  • Calculate real-time ETAs and distances to assigned retail hubs.
  • Map daily optimized delivery and field visit routes (*e.g., Route North-A Premium*).
  • Verify geo-fenced shop check-ins when logging visits or placing orders.
  • Track field agents on live operational maps for distribution tracking.

B. Camera & Photo Storage Access

We request access to CAMERA and device photo gallery/storage permissions. This data is used to:

  • Profile Picture Upload: Allow users to capture or select an image to personalize their account profile avatar.
  • Bill & Stamp Verification: Capture photos of stamped physical bills, order collection slips, or delivery proofs.
  • Payment Receipts: Upload image evidence for cash, cheque, or bank transfer payments collected on-site.

C. Account & Retailer Information

  • Sales Partner Details: Registered mobile phone number, login credentials, agent name, and account avatar.
  • Retailer Data: Shop name, owner contact numbers, business address, GSTIN, and newly onboarded store credentials.
  • Transaction Records: Invoices, payment mode logs (Cash, Cheque, Bank, UPI), and customer outstanding balance records.

2. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or distribute your personal details, profile picture, or location data to third-party advertisers. Data is shared exclusively with:

  • Distribution Admins & Hub Managers: Enterprise portal managers monitoring daily route progress and payment collections.
  • Infrastructure Providers: Secure cloud database hosts, map provider APIs (e.g., Google Maps), and SMS OTP gateways essential for app operations.
  • Legal Compliance: When mandated by legal or administrative regulatory bodies.

3. Managing Your Permissions & Data Rights

Users can manage or revoke camera, gallery, and location permissions at any time through their device's system settings (Settings > Apps > VK Marketing > Permissions). Please note that revoking continuous location access may prevent you from starting delivery routes or logging shop visits.

To request deletion of your account profile picture or account data, contact your hub administrator or reach out to our support team.

4. Security Standards

All transmitted location coordinates, uploaded images, and financial transactions are encrypted in transit using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols to prevent unauthorized access or interception.
